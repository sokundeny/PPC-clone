Phnom Penh Crown Football Club

Overview

RSN Stadium is named after the club owner, Rithy Samnang. It was opened in 2015 and a maximum capacity is 5000. It was changed to Smart RSN Stadium in 2019 after Smart Axiata company becoming the title sponsor.

Club name:

Phnom Penh Crown FC

Year of Foundation:

2001

President:

Mrs. Phu Cherlin

Home Stadium:

Smart RSN Stadium

Nick name:

PPCFC, The RED Singha, The RED Lion

Our Goal

Within 4 years to have a club with a solid fan base, strong community attachment, and sufficient talent to be competitive in AFC and regional competitions. Phnom Penh Crown → Phnom Penh → Cambodia → Southeast Asia.
